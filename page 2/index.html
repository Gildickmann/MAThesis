יש threshold effect. הקפיצה הגדולה: לא ראה → ראה חלקי, לא בין חלקי למלא. לאשמה וחרטה, חשיפה חלקית מייצרת שינוי גדול יותר מחשיפה מלאה — עקבי עם Moral Shock Theory. לאחריות ביבי — יש accumulation.

BH-significant count: Ordinal vs Binary
Why ordinal is more sensitive
Binary collapses partial and full exposure into one group. But from Q3 we know:
— Partial exposure: Δguilty=+0.53, Δregret=+0.44
— Full exposure: Δguilt=+0.21, Δregret=+0.08

Ordinal uses this within-exposed variance. Binary discards it. Outcomes that benefit most from this information (behavioral actions, policy attitudes) are exactly the ones that drop out in binary.

Negative controls unchanged: 0/4 BH-sig in both — the specificity argument holds regardless of coding.
